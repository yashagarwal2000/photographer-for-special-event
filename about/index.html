About

Yash Agarwal is photographer who is on the rise of popularity when it comes to his black and white portiats of everyday people. For over 14 years. Yash has traveled in 6 different countries to capture the picturesque views from different walks of life. Many admire his black and white portiats. "I have always been intrigued with capturing moments, small moments can showcase one of the biggest moments and telling stories." "I believe I was 7 years old, first introduced to photography." "Ever since then I have been passionate with photography. "
